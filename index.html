What is utfive

What is
utfive

utfive is a news app that provides credible news stories from different interest areas in the form of articles, videos, and images which are manually curated and verified by our team and are supported with at least two reliable sources as a mark of trust which provide a better sense of reliability to you and let you understand different perspectives of a news story. This is our step towards empowering you, the common people with a medium like utfive so that you can think critically about important topics and eventually, you can eliminate fake news, misinformation, and biased news from your lives.

Unique Features

utfive keeps you well-informed with the help of following features.

Multiple Sources for Every News

Manually Curated and Verified News
